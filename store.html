Merchandise with Meaning

We're developing a collection of products that not only spread awareness about dolphin intelligence and conservation but also directly support our research and protection efforts. Every purchase will contribute to the dolphin singularity mission.

Expected Launch: Early 2025

We're taking time to ensure our products are:

  • Sustainably sourced - Ocean-friendly materials and production
  • Ethically made - Fair labor practices throughout the supply chain
  • High quality - Products that last and represent our values
  • Mission-aligned - Each item tells the story of dolphin conservation
