Did You Know?

Dolphins have the longest social memory of any non-human species. Research shows they can remember the signature whistles of other dolphins they haven't encountered for over 20 years - longer than elephants!

Source: Proceedings of the Royal Society B, 2013

Frequently Asked Questions

Are all resources really free to use?

Yes, absolutely! All our educational materials, research datasets, and advocacy tools are completely free to download and use. We only ask that you provide attribution when sharing or publishing work based on our resources. Our mission is to advance dolphin conservation through open knowledge sharing.

Can I use these resources for commercial purposes?

Educational and research materials are licensed under Creative Commons BY-NC-SA, meaning you can use them for non-commercial purposes with attribution. If you're interested in commercial applications (such as textbook publishing or commercial software), please contact us for licensing arrangements. All proceeds support our conservation research.

How often are resources updated?

We update existing resources quarterly to reflect the latest research findings. New resources are added monthly. Major curriculum updates occur annually. Subscribers to our educator newsletter receive notifications about all updates and new releases, plus early access to beta versions of new tools.

What technical requirements are needed for the analysis tools?

Our software tools are designed to run on standard computers. Minimum requirements: Windows 10/macOS 10.14 or later, 8GB RAM, 2GB free storage. For processing large acoustic datasets, we recommend 16GB RAM. Python 3.8+ is required. Detailed installation guides and troubleshooting support are included with each download.

How can I contribute my own research or teaching materials?

We welcome contributions from the community! Submit your materials through our contact form with a brief description. Our editorial team reviews submissions for scientific accuracy and educational value. Contributors are credited and acknowledged on resource pages. High-quality contributions may be featured in our monthly newsletter.

Is technical support available?

Yes! We offer community support through our forum where users and developers can help each other. For technical issues with our software tools, email support is available with typical response times of 24-48 hours. Premium support with priority response is available for institutional users and research collaborators.
