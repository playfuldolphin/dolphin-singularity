Current Research

Vocal Signatures

Each dolphin develops a unique signature whistle - essentially their name. AI is helping us understand how these signatures function in complex social networks.

Syntax & Grammar

Evidence suggests dolphin communication may have grammatical structure. Machine learning models are analyzing sequences to identify potential syntactic patterns.

Cognitive Mapping

Research into how dolphins perceive and communicate about their environment, revealing remarkable spatial awareness and information sharing capabilities.

The Singularity Hypothesis

As AI technology advances, we approach a moment where human-dolphin communication could become truly bidirectional. This "dolphin singularity" represents not just a technological breakthrough, but a fundamental shift in our relationship with non-human intelligence.

Key Conservation Issues

About Dolphin Singularity

Dolphin Singularity is dedicated to exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and dolphin communication. We believe that understanding and communicating with dolphins represents one of humanity's most important frontiers - both scientifically and ethically.

Our Mission

To advance the understanding of dolphin intelligence through AI technology, supporting both scientific research and conservation efforts that protect these remarkable beings.

Our Vision

A future where humans and dolphins can communicate meaningfully, leading to greater respect, protection, and coexistence between species.

Research & Conservation Partners

This work builds on groundbreaking research from the DolphinGemma project and supports the conservation mission of The Dolphin Project, founded by Ric O'Barry.
