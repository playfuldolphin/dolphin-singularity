Conservation Efforts

Protecting Dolphin Populations

The Dolphin Project and conservation partners worldwide work to protect dolphins from captivity, hunting, and environmental threats. Understanding dolphin intelligence through AI research strengthens the case for their protection.

Key Conservation Issues

  • Captivity: Ending the exploitation of dolphins in marine parks and entertainment facilities
  • Hunting & Bycatch: Stopping deliberate hunting and reducing fishing net casualties
  • Habitat Protection: Preserving ocean ecosystems and reducing pollution
  • Climate Change: Addressing impacts on marine environments and food sources

How You Can Help

  • Support The Dolphin Project's conservation work
  • Avoid facilities that keep dolphins in captivity
  • Reduce ocean plastic pollution
  • Support sustainable seafood practices
  • Advocate for marine protected areas
  • Share research and raise awareness
